About This Tool

Upload a QuickSource HTML file from TOTAL to automatically check prior sales disclosure requirements:

  • Subject property: Any transfers in the 3 years prior to the effective date
  • Comparable sales: Any transfers in the 12 months prior to the date of sale
  • Pending period: Transfers between contract and settlement (unusual)

You'll get a copy-paste ready paragraph for your report plus detailed transfer information.
